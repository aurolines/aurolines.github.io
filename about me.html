Hi, I'm Divyansh Maghanani — a BBA student, published author, and aspiring marketer.

I find stories in silence, patterns in data, and purpose in everything I create. Whether it's publishing heartfelt books like The Heart That Holds On or The Ink That Fades, or working behind the scenes building backlinks and tracking keyword ranks, I enjoy connecting emotion with execution.
