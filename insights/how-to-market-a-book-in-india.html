Zero-Budget Playbook

How to Market Your Book in India — Zero Budget

Actionable tactics for KDP, Pothi, Goodreads, Instagram and SEO. No ad spend required.

Published Nov 2025 • Reading time: ~8–10 min

Foundation — Make Your Book Discoverable

Metadata, keywords and SEO are your permanent assets.

Invest time in product-level SEO: titles, subtitles, seven keywords on KDP, correct categories and a clear description that answers reader intent.

Amazon KDP

Pick 7 keywords, 2 precise categories. Use long-tail phrases in your description.

Pothi (India POD)

Upload print-on-demand variant for India delivery — no inventory cost in print-on-order mode.

Goodreads

Claim author page, add books, participate in groups — social proof matters.

Social — Instagram & LinkedIn Playbook

Reels, carousels and stories that actually convert interest into readers.

Consistency beats polish. Recycle the same content into: reels, carousel posts, story snippets and LinkedIn carousels.

Reels

3 reels/week: BTS + voiceover poetry lines + writing process clips.

Carousel

“5 lessons from writing my book” — evergreen for LinkedIn + Instagram.

Stories

Pinned reviews, BTS, early bird links and Q&A highlights.

Micro-Influencer Strategy

Real reviews without paid promotion.

Target 1k–20k followers (book micro-influencers). Provide a free ebook + ready-made assets (quote pack) to reduce friction.

Find reviewers

Search hashtags, book lists, bookstagram accounts and small blogs.

Make sharing easy

Send a short pitch, sample chapter and suggested post copy.

SEO — The long-term machine

Pillar + cluster, internal linking and search intent — compound traffic over time.

This page is Page 2 in your cluster. Use Page 1 and this page as a pillar pair and link to niche supporting posts (cover design, publishing checklist, email list growth).

Pillar & Cluster

One pillar article + 3–5 supporting pages linked internally.

On-page

H1 with main keyword, answer user intent early, add FAQ schema if useful.

Tracking

Search Console + GA4 weekly snapshots — track queries, CTR and pages that convert to Amazon/Pothi clicks.

30-Day Zero-Budget Starter Plan

Weekly milestones you can implement now.

  1. Week 1: Publish pillar + supporting post; share on LinkedIn & Medium.
  2. Week 2: Upload Pothi listing; claim Goodreads author profile; post launch content.
  3. Week 3: Reach out to 10 micro-reviewers with ebook + assets.
  4. Week 4: Run reels; gather reviews; upload SlideShare PDF for backlinks.
